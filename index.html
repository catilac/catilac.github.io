Hello! My name is Chirag Davé (she/they). I am a freelance software engineer generalist, apple developer, and Metal graphics programmer. I’ve worked with startups for 15 years at companies of different sizes, and some of my own. I’ve been the 1st engineering hire, 8th, and 80th (or something). While I primarily flourish as an IC, I also enjoy doing engineering and product management.

I love thinking about code, how we build things, and why. I believe the right design, from mockups to software architecture, can lead to a revolution in the mind of the end user. I find the most interesting software can expand a person's capabilities and imagination, allowing for possibilities even the creator would not have considered.

In the past I worked with WeTransfer, on their Paper app. In the Fall of 2019 I attended The Recurse Center where I made a live shader editor called PsychOut, and collaborated on CARL (Code AR Live) for which we were awarded a grant.
Further in the past, I was the second engineering hire at Grove (acquired by Wealthfront), worked on web, iOS, and search teams at Rdio (Acquired by Pandora), founded 2 startups, and was first engineering hire at UserVoice.

For the past year, I’ve focused on making music and performing. I’m now attending Recurse Center for the second time to focus on learning DSP for VST plugin development, and embedded systems (think guitar pedals and hardware synthesizers).

My favorite languages are Swift, C, Go, and Javascript.

My last clients involved doing work such as:

  • GPU pipeline optimization and shader implementation for an animation that responded to blood pressure readings taken by the camera in real time
  • Feature development for novel film editing software, code auditing, setting up tools for development by multiple engineers, and hiring a product manager
  • Porting a real time audio pitch detection library from C to swift, and then optimizing the swift code to perform just as efficiently as the C code
  • Helped refactor an iOS codebase built by an old school javascript developer to best practices, and using SwiftUI
  • Building MacOS apps using the Go programming language
