Hello! My name is Chirag Davé (she/they). I am a freelance iOS engineer and Metal graphics programmer. I am currently attending Recurse Center for the second time to focus on learning DSP for plugin development, and embedded systems. I love thinking about code, how we build things, and why. I believe the right design, from mockups to software architecture, can lead to a revolution in the mind of the end user. I find the most interesting software can expand a person's capabilities and imagination, allowing for possibilities even the creator would not have considered.

Some testemonials: Chirag is phenomenal on many levels: incredibly kind and enjoyable to collaborate with, able to achieve the impossible, and with a keen eye for both performance and quality of experience. I strongly recommend working with Chirag! Riva Health had a very ambitious animation design (motion + visual) that needed to run on GPU alongside other intense GPU-based processing. Going into the project, we were afraid the animation would compromise the quality of our signal processing or take away overhead from future GPU needs. Chirag was pulled into the project late, but immediately helped us understand and de-risk how we could pull off the UX we were looking for. As with any design<>developer handoff, there's always concern that the design will be compromised or miss the mark of what the design team intended (yet knew was overly ambitious). With Chirag's help, we were able to capture the true essence and emotive qualities of the design in the end result and the entire company from CEO downwards said it was "their favorite app update" by far. Chirag went even further by optimizing code performance so our research teams would have plenty of GPU overhead to work with. This speaks to not only his care for the craft of UX, but also to the needs of being responsible with device resources—a quality not many developers can achieve in balance. We look forward to working with Chirag on future efforts and recommend you do the same!
